About

Partnership

John Cook, Executive Editor

John Cook is the executive editor of Gawker Media. Prior to that, he was the company’s executive editor for investigations, working with staff across Gawker Media’s eight sites to strategize and generate high-impact stories. He has been the editor-in-chief of First Look Media’s The Intercept as well as the editor-in-chief of Gawker.com, where, among other things, he broke the story of Toronto Mayor Rob Ford’s crack cocaine use. He has worked as a reporter or editor at Radar Magazine, The Chicago Tribune, Mother Jones, Yahoo! News, and Brill’s Content, and is the co-author of Our Noise: The Story of Merge Records.

Nick Denton, Founder & CEO

Nick Denton is founder of Gawker Media. Before building a media business out of blogs, Nick founded two internet ventures in the late 1990s: Moreover Technologies, the news search provider, acquired by Verisign; and First Tuesday. Nick — who is half-Hungarian — began his media career as a reporter for the Economist and the Financial Times during the fall of communism in Eastern Europe. While the FT's investment banking correspondent he co-wrote All That Glitters, the account of the rogue trader who brought down Barings Bank.

Heather Dietrick, President & General Counsel

Heather Dietrick is President & General Counsel of Gawker Media and oversees all things legal. Before joining Gawker, Heather worked in-house at a multinational media company, clerked in the Eastern District of New York, and practiced as an intellectual property and media attorney for two of New York’s leading law firms. Heather holds a JD/MBA from the University of Michigan. She is a fast walker and a fierce defender of the First Amendment.

Ian Fette, Chief Technology Officer

Ian Fette is Gawker Media's CTO, overseeing the engineering organization that keeps the bits and bytes flowing. Prior to joining Gawker, Ian spent eight years at Google, both as a product manager and engineering manager. His experience spans across consumer products like Chrome, to behind-the-scenes work like managing privacy and security on Gmail. Prior to Google, Ian worked as an engineer for the Department of Defense. Ian has degrees in computer science from Michigan and Carnegie Mellon. When not at work, you'll find Ian in a plane - usually in the pilot's seat.

Josh Albertson, Chief Operating Officer

Josh Albertson is Chief Operating Officer of Gawker Media. Most recently, Josh was a vice president at Vox Media, where he led business development and revenue operations. Prior to Vox, he was president of Curbed Network, which he helped build from a single local blog into a leading digital publisher with a national footprint and three influential brands — Curbed, Eater & Racked. Earlier in his career, Josh reported on the first wave of the Internet boom for Smartmoney.com. In his college days, he was the editor-in-chief of The Brown Daily Herald.

Mia Libby, SVP, Global Sales & Partnerships

Mia Libby is Senior Vice President of Global Sales and Partnerships at Gawker Media where she manages the national sales and studio@gawker team. Previously, she led the national sales team. Before joining Gawker, Mia worked at Conde Nast at WIRED and WIRED.com. Mia graduated from Hamilton College and lives with her husband and daughter in Westchester.
